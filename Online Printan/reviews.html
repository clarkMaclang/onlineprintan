FAQs

Q: How to make an order? How to order a sticker? How to order a tarpaulin?
A: You can go to our “How to Order” page to have a detailed instructions on how to make an order,
for both sticker and tarpaulin.

Q: What are the modes of payment? Is COD (Cash On Deliver) availabe?
A: We offer different kinds of payments, you can view it when you are about to check out your order/s.
We offer COD, Bank Transfer, selected online payment brand.

Q: What file formats do you accept for printing?
A: We accept pdf, png, jpeg, and psd file formats for your designs.

Q: How much does shipping cost? How long does it take to deliver?
A: The shipping cost and the time of delivery is dependent to your location.

Q: What sizes are available for tarpaulins and stickers?
A: You can view are chart, located at the topmost right of the website, to see the available sizes
for tarpaulins and stickers.

Q: Can I cancel my order? Can I make changes to my order after placing it?
A: You are not allowed to cancel your orders. However, you can change it, just click the change button
and we will email you for updates if we are able to change it.
