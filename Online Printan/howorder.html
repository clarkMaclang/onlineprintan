HOW TO ORDER?

1. BROWSE AND SELECT SERVICE
Navigate to the Services section in the main menu. Choose the specific printing service you need from the options, either tarpaulin or sticker.

2. ENTER ORDER DETAILS ON SERVICE PAGE
In the service form, specify details for your print, such as:
Size: Select or input the dimensions (Width and Length) for your print.
Upload Image: Use the upload button to submit your design or any required files.

3. PREVIEW AND REVIEW ORDER
Click on the Preview button to see how your print will look. Check all details carefully, including dimensions, design, and any other customization to ensure accuracy.

4. ADD TO CART
Once satisfied with the preview, click on Order to add the item to your cart.

5. PROCEED TO CHECKOUT
Go to your Cart by clicking on the cart icon in the header. Review your order summary, which will display the items in your cart, the total cost, and any applicable taxes or discounts.

6. CONFIRM AND PAY
Click on the Checkout button. Select a payment method and provide the required payment details. Confirm your order by clicking Confirm Payment. After successful payment, you will receive an order confirmation and tracking information.

7. TRACK ORDER
We will update you about the order through your email and number.

8. PROVIDE FEEDBACK
Once your order is completed and delivered, visit the Reviews section to provide feedback. You can also upload images of the printed product if desired.
